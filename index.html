My dearest Baby Iah,

Five months. It doesn't sound like a lot when you say it out loud — but in my heart, it feels like I've known you in every lifetime I've ever lived. Like somehow, the universe had been quietly arranging everything just so we could find each other in this one.

You are the kind of rare and gentle magic that people write poems about but rarely get to hold. And I get to hold you. Every single day, I think about how lucky that makes me — how lucky it makes us.

things I love about you, a short but endless list —

  • The way you exist so softly, yet you fill every room you walk into.
  • How your laugh sounds like the first breath of spring after a long winter.
  • The warmth you carry — it doesn't just light up a space, it heals it.
  • Your eyes when you're happy. I keep that sight like a secret treasure.
  • The little things you do that you don't even notice — I notice all of them.
  • How you make ordinary moments feel like something worth remembering forever.

These five months have been my favorite story. Not because everything was perfect — but because even in the hard moments, I always wanted to be right here, beside you. I'd choose the difficult with you over the easy without you, every single time.

I don't know what the next five months hold, or the five after that, or all the months that come after that — but I know that I want every single one of them to be with you. You are my home, Baby. My softest place in this very loud world.

Happy 5th monthsary. I love you — more than these little words could ever carry, but I'll keep trying to show you anyway.

✦

yours, completely and quietly,

Your Baby 🌸
