My dearest Iah,

Five months. It doesn't sound like a lot when you say it out loud, but for me, it feels like one of the best times of my life.

You are the kind of rare and pretty girl that people write poems about but rarely get to hold. And I get to hold you. Every single day, I think about how lucky I am to have you in my life, especially you're a supportive girlfriend.

things I love about you, a short but endless list:

  • Your voice that comforts me in my silent battles.
  • Your smile that makes me feel better throughout the day.
  • Your goofiness that makes me laugh even when I'm feeling a little down.
  • Your sparkling eyes that when you look at me, it is a full of emotions.
  • The little things you do that you don't even notice. Simple gestures like how you care about what I eat, what I do, and more
  • Your presence that motivates me to do better every day.

These months since we talked again, have been my favorite months. Not because everything was perfect, but, because even in the hard moments, I can easily pass with you supporting me and motivating me. After the hard work, I'm very excited to say how's my day with you, you're like my human diary and I hope I can be yours too. I just want to remind you that you also have me. You may have silent battles but I do hope you'll depend on me when you really need someone.

I don't know what the next five months hold, or the five after that, or all the months that come after that, but, I know that I want every single one of them to be with you. I would cherish all my memories with you forevery by! You are my pahinga, Baby. My softest place in this very loud world.

Happy 5th monthsary by! Just know that I love you so much than these little words could ever carry, if you cannot feel my love with my letter, it's okay since I'll keep trying.

✦

yours, completely and quietly,

Charles 🌸
